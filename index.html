Basic Commission From Hosts

Basic commission is calculated from host basic income and the agency commission rate.

Core Formulas

  • Basic Commission = Host Basic Income x Commission Rate
  • Host Basic Income = Host Gift Income + Host Call Income
  • Today's commission rate is determined by host earnings from the past 30 days.

Valid income includes all host basic income and all agency self income. Host basic income counts hosts who belong to you and hosts under all invited agents in your agency link.

Commission From Invited Agencies

Sub-agency commission is calculated from the difference between your commission rate and the sub-agency's commission rate.

Sub Agency Commission = Sub Agency Host Basic Income x (Your Commission Rate - Sub Agency Commission Rate)

Talent Host Policy

The Talent Host Program gives qualified hosts more exposure, profile benefits, traffic support, and a path to Star Host or S-idol status.

Talented Host

Benefits

  • Weekly salary up to $20.
  • Exclusive tag on the profile page.
  • Exclusive live room badge with traffic boost.
  • Chance to become a Star Host and earn weekly salary up to $60.

Application Requirements

  • Hosts must have completed at least 30 minutes of independent live streaming.
  • Hosts must have received more than 100 diamonds during live stream.
  • Applications can be submitted only when program slots are available.
  • The application video must be at least 30 seconds and clearly demonstrate talent.
1

Video Review

No special effects or heavy filters. Video quality must be clear, stable, properly framed, and aligned with the selected talent category.

2

Salary Rules

After approval, the host salary upgrades to Talent Host salary from the following Monday at 00:00 UTC+8. Up to 3 live hours per day count toward salary tasks.

3

Retention & Elimination

Hosts must meet weekly Lucky Gift earnings or gifting-user thresholds. If not, Talent Host status is removed next week and salary returns to the basic Live salary plan.

From this policy onward, Star Hosts and S-idol hosts must be promoted from Talent Hosts. Direct Star Host applications are no longer accepted. Leave requests must be reported to the assigned admin before Monday 00:00 UTC+8.

Core Rules

  • Maximum counted live time is 4 hours per day.
  • Salary is paid weekly, with maximum weekly salary of $110.
  • Tasks refresh at 00:00:00 UTC+8 on the first day of each week, and live duration is counted from that time.
  • Each completed task can be claimed for the corresponding reward.

Application & Live Requirements

  • Participated in at least one livehouse event or completed a 20-minute solo live audition.
  • Send a talent video of at least 15 seconds. Singing, dancing, and other talents are accepted.
  • Contact the agency admin or official email: star.host@iboloupapp.com.
  • Use a qualified live cover, avatar, and nickname; keep clean appearance, proper camera distance, lighting, background music, and active interaction.
Cancellation Rules:

Fraud or regulation violations will immediately terminate the fixed salary policy and trigger penalties. Star Hosts with negative performance will be warned; three warnings within one month will cancel Star Host status.
